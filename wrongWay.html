Project information

    Wrong Way" is a 2D endless runner mobile game developed in Unity, exclusively for Android. Players navigate a vehicle driving against traffic on a one-way street using the device's accelerometer to steer the car left or right. The objective is to avoid oncoming cars and obstacles for as long as possible to achieve a high score. The game features various obstacles, including oncoming vehicles, roadblocks, and dynamic elements like pedestrians. Players can collect power-ups, such as shields, speed boosts, and slow-downs, to aid in their survival. The scoring system rewards distance traveled and near-misses with other cars. As players progress, the difficulty increases with more frequent and faster oncoming cars. The game includes a main menu, pause menu, and game over screen, along with background music and sound effects. "Wrong Way" is optimized for smooth performance on a range of Android devices, ensuring an engaging and challenging gaming experience.
